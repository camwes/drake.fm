TLDR; you are invited to 505 Grove st EVERY Thursday evening for drinking, chilling and stimulating conversation.

I know a lot of dope people. If you are reading this, you are probably one of them. The best thing about knowing so many cool people is getting them together in the same room and observing the outcome. In an effort to create as many of these situations as possible we (Dan, Marcus and Cameron) are hosting a Thursday night salon, for the intermingling of minds, the imbibing of booze and the listening of music. You are encouraged to bring something to share (a vinyl to play, a bottle to drink, a friend, etc.) or just yourself. We only ask that you limit distractions, which means if your phone is out for longer than a host (or guest) deems necessary it could be confiscated.

The inspiration, if you are unfamiliar are the great salons of the French literary and artistic renaissance. From Wikipedia: "A salon is a gathering of people under the roof of an inspiring host, held partly to amuse one another and partly to refine the taste and increase the knowledge of the participants through conversation. These gatherings often consciously followed Horace's definition of the aims of poetry, "either to please or to educate" ("aut delectare aut prodesse est")."
